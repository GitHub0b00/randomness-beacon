The beacon of randomness and trust.


What you have here is a service that presents 512 digits of random bits of zeros and ones every minute. The random value is presented in the data field outputValue using hexidecimal format. The randomness came from two sources of commercial Quantum Random Number Generator by "S-15". The web randomness service aims to be a trusted beacon of randomness. The service is built in the "SpooQy Lab" at Center for Quantum Technology, National University of Singapore. The service is now operating and being supervised under Singapore's National Quantum-Safe Network program.

In addition to the random string every minute, this web application provides random bits streaming on demand. The random bits streaming service is independent from the beacon service in two ways. First, the beacon service referenced to the NIST's reference for building a randomness beacon, and provides random strings on a fixed interval. Whereas the streaming service aims to provide the most real-time randomness without a formating framework. Second, the streaming serviceand beacon service ultilizes different random number generation methods. The beacon service uses Homodyne Detection of Quantum Vacuum Fluctuation as thesource of random bits. Whereas the streaming service uses another quantum phenomenon caused by the interaction of single photon and beam splitter to generate the random bits.
